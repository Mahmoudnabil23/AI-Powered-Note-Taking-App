Q4 Marketing Strategy

Created by You
• Last edited 2 mins ago

The primary goal for Q4 is to expand our user base by 15%25% through targeted social media campaigns and strategic partnerships. We need to focus on high-conversion channels identified in the Q3 audit.

add_circle Key Initiatives

  • Launch the "Refer a Friend" program by Nov 1st.
  • Optimize landing pages for mobile traffic (currently 60% of visitors).
  • Collaborate with 3 key influencers in the tech space.

Budget allocation will be adjusted to prioritize these initiatives. We will reduce print media spend by 50%. We will reallocate 50% of print media budget to digital ads. This shift aligns with our new digital-first approach.

Metrics for success include CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), LTV (Lifetime Value), and monthly active users. We will review these metrics bi-weekly.
